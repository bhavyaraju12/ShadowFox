Our Legacy ↓

Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) has built a legacy of resilience, tactical brilliance, and unwavering focus on excellence since their IPL debut in 2013. Renowned for their formidable bowling attacks, led by stars like Bhuvneshwar Kumar and Rashid Khan, SRH has redefined defending low totals, highlighted by their IPL title win in 2016.

Beyond cricket, SRH is deeply committed to nurturing young talent and fostering future stars, providing a platform for growth and success. Off the field, their impactful community initiatives reflect a dedication to giving back, inspiring fans and making a difference. SRH’s legacy extends beyond their achievements, embodying excellence, unity, and a spirit of contribution to cricket and society.

Moreover, their commitment to community initiatives and philanthropic endeavors underscores their ethos of giving back and making a meaningful difference beyond the boundary ropes. In essence, the legacy of the Sunrisers transcends mere cricketing achievements; it's a testament to their indomitable spirit and enduring legacy in the realm of Indian cricket.

Our Legends

  • David Warner

    One of SRH's greatest players, Warner was instrumental in their 2016 IPL title victory. As captain and a prolific opener, he consistently delivered with the bat, winning the Orange Cap thrice (2015, 2017, 2019). His leadership and aggressive batting defined SRH’s competitive spirit.

  • Bhuvneshwar Kumar

    A cornerstone of SRH’s formidable bowling attack, Bhuvneshwar is known for his ability to swing the ball and deliver under pressure. He won the Purple Cap (highest wicket-taker) in back-to-back seasons (2016 and 2017). His contributions have made SRH synonymous with bowling excellence.

  • Rashid Khan

    The Afghan spinner became SRH’s X-factor with his unmatched skill and consistency in T20 cricket. Known for his economical bowling and wicket-taking ability, Rashid played a key role in helping SRH maintain their reputation as a top bowling side. His match-winning performances made him a fan favorite and an SRH icon.
